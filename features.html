What is Qlipper?

Qlipper is a private AI video editor designed for creators who want a repeatable workflow for turning long-form videos into short clips. It helps you find moments, frame them for vertical platforms, and export consistently.

PRIVACY

Local-first workflow

Keep your source videos under your control. Build content without feeling locked into cloud-only tools.

SPEED

Designed to move fast

A workflow that minimizes waiting: less uploading, less context switching, more publishing.

OUTPUT

Short-form ready

Export formats for TikTok / Reels / Shorts with consistent framing and styling.
